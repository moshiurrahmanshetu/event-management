The Founder

Sophia Laurent

❈

With over fifteen years of bespoke event composition across Italy, France, and Switzerland, Sophia Laurent has established herself as a leading visionary of the experiential luxury movement. Her design philosophy revolves around spatial poetics—mapping how natural sunset rays stream through historic columns and aligning linen coordinates to frame ancient limestone walls.

"True luxury is found in absolute stillness. When a client walks into a grand ballroom, their breathing should ease, guided by a sensory landscape that feels entirely natural yet flawlessly structured."

Sophia scouts every destination personally, vetting Michelin-starred culinary partnerships and coordinating acoustic engineers to verify the decibel propagation of classical string quartets inside historical cathedral vaults.

The Lead Planner

Marcus Vance

❈

If Sophia Laurent is our creative composer, Marcus Vance is the technical orchestrator. Backed by a master's degree in architectural operations and years of large-scale staging, Marcus ensures our visual masterpieces exist securely in physical reality. He manages multi-currency custom budgets, international customs approvals for exotic botanical shipments, and strict 72-hour installation blueprints.

The 72-Hour Orchestration Process
T-MINUS 48 HOURS • INFRASTRUCTURE

Custom flooring overlays are anchored, power distribution is tested for silent hum, and major floral armatures are rigged securely in vaulted rafters.

T-MINUS 24 HOURS • SENSORY SEEDING

Table glassware is hand-polished, acoustics are mapped relative to crowd-density expectations, and dynamic lighting presets are locked relative to hourly sun forecasts.

T-MINUS 2 HOURS • CHOREOGRAPHY RUNS

Full technical dry-runs with audio engineering, catering cues, and pyrotechnic crews are synchronized to sub-second precision.

The Master Photographer

Elena Rostova

❈

A graduate of École Nationale Supérieure de la Photographie in Arles, Elena Rostova captures our commissions with an editorial, haute-couture eye. Her shots are regularly featured in international luxury bridal journals, praised for their high-contrast depth, classical composition lines, and organic, non-staged emotional weight.

Behind The Lens: Recent Captures (Hover / Idle Transitions)

Elena exclusively handles 35mm physical film cameras and high-precision medium format Leica systems, capturing color depth and shadow textures that digital sensors fail to resolve natively.

The Floral & Scenography Designer

Isabella Reed

❈

Isabella Reed converts physical architectural layouts into lush botanical stories. Believing that floral design must relate to the history of the site, she references local flora of each destination—pairing wild olive boughs and heavy garden roses in Tuscany, or structural lavender lines and dry stone elements in southern France.
