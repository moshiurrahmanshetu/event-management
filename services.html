Atelier Core Sphere

01. End-to-End Complete Wedding Planning

Our flagship complete planning service takes you on an stress-free journey from initial design parameters to your grand send-off. We manage the complex web of vendors, agreements, budgets, and guest registrations so you remain present to enjoy every milestone moment.

Through real-time logistics software, structured timeline matrices, and dedicated on-site event coordination, we ensure your wedding is choregraphed down to the second.

  • Custom 12-month progress checklists and vendor integration maps
  • Financial matrix management and contract review safeguards
  • On-site production team and timing directors (up to 24 hours of live management)
  • Post-event wrap up, supplier dispatching, and logistics resolution
Global Escapes

02. Destination Wedding Curation

From private Amalfi Coast villas and historic châteaux in southern France to the iconic whitewashed cliffs of Santorini, our international planning division secures exclusive destination access worldwide.

We handle the intricate details of legal filings, overseas registry processing, luxury guest accommodations, and specialized regional transport solutions.

  • Bespoke location scouting, site inspections, and local tax filings
  • Foreign registry translation management and legal consultant coordination
  • Guest concierge services, lodging bookings, and luxury transfer assets
  • Multi-day itinerary planning (welcome sunset cocktail, brunch, private yacht sails)
Visual Preservation

03. Fine Art Photography & Cinema

We collaborate with award-winning editorial film photographers and cinematic directors to capture your celebration with visual poetry. Rather than standard snapshots, we prioritize light balance, romantic angles, and raw emotion.

Every file is processed in custom boutique studios, yielding physical, luxury leather-bound albums and raw cinematic footage that preserve your legacy.

  • Fine art medium-format film and digital ultra-high-definition capture
  • Bespoke wedding drone photography and cinematic aerial coordination
  • Private digital portfolios and luxury physical leather-bound memory books
  • Engagement sessions and pre-wedding rehearsal film portraits
Creative Styling

04. Custom Decoration & Botanical Styling

Led by our master stylists, we outline a unique, couture aesthetic DNA for your event. We draft specialized color stories, select linen textures, and model asymmetrical botanical arches that complement your venue's inherent architecture.

Our florists source premium, fresh seasonal blossoms globally to assemble breathtaking living backdrops and elegant tabletop centerpieces.

  • 3D spatial layout modeling and high-fidelity rendering maps
  • Couture linen sourcing, curated charger plates, and bespoke dinnerware
  • Fresh, premium global floral imports and sculptural botanical walls
  • Atmospheric and fairy-light custom electrical placement design
Estate Scouting

05. Exclusive Venue Selection & Scouting

Finding the ideal stage is half the magic. Aurelia holds proprietary access partnerships with elite global estates, historical landmarks, private islands, and luxury villas that are generally inaccessible to the public.

We evaluate physical acoustics, golden-hour shadow metrics, weather contingency options, and vendor ingress capabilities to ensure your venue is perfectly functional.

  • Proprietary access catalogs for premium private properties globally
  • Detailed venue site survey logs, acoustics audits, and shadow map planning
  • Exclusive negotiations, insurance checkups, and rental clearances
  • Full multi-day venue occupancy logistics and layout modeling
Couture Fashion

06. Bride & Groom Styling Concierge

Ensure your attire is as timeless as your vows. Our elite fashion consultants assist with dress scouting, luxury tailor bookings, and bespoke designer fittings in Milan, Paris, and London.

We coordinate bridal dress preservation, on-site steamed styling prep on your celebration morning, and bespoke styling details for the bridal party.

  • Private salon bookings, custom sketch approvals, and trunk show access
  • Master Italian tailor sourcing for custom groom suits and tuxedos
  • On-site styling assistants and dress steamers on celebration day
  • Bridal party fashion planning and custom cosmetic trial bookings
Audiovisual Arts

07. Symphony Orchestra & Live Entertainment

Evoke raw emotion through curated sound. We coordinate with elite classical string quartets, symphony orchestra directors, world-class jazz vocalists, and high-energy luxury reception bands.

Our production team installs custom sound architecture, spectacular programmed fairy-light transitions, and bespoke projection systems.

  • Exclusive bookings for high-profile artists, bands, and international performers
  • Programmed orchestral soundscapes for processional vows and toasts
  • Custom spatial acoustic designs and premium speaker installations
  • Programmed light shows, fireworks, and outdoor sparkler arrays
Gastronomical Mastery

08. Michelin-Caliber Catering & Wine Curation

Treat your guests to a sensory culinary voyage. We partner with Michelin-rated executive chefs to draft custom gastronomic menus, elegant molecular dessert structures, and custom cocktail formulations.

Our sommelier division reviews private vineyards to hand-select champagne vintages and premium wine pairings that complement every bite.

  • Custom menu prototyping, private chef-led tastings, and plating plans
  • Premium molecular dessert counters and bespoke tiered cake architectures
  • Exclusive champagne imports and aged vintage red/white wine pairing guides
  • Staffing and service choreography (white-glove butler services)
Corporate Curation

09. High-Profile Corporate Galas & Summits

We translate brand narratives into high-end physical experiences. Whether organizing a luxury charity banquet, an architectural product launch, or a private executive board dinner, we enforce strict logistical precision.

Our designers build immersive branded zones, press walls, and secure coordinates for high-profile stakeholders and global media networks.

  • Complete brand styling, immersive media screens, and press room maps

  • High-end sound systems, translators, and presentation technologies
