Our Story

An Unwavering Pursuit of Pure Aesthetic Perfection

Aurelia was born from a singular, uncompromising vision: that weddings should be treated with the same meticulous curation and architectural rigor as high-fashion runway shows or grand institutional galas. Founded by lead designer Sophia Laurent, the atelier began as a small boutique planning service in Milan.

We recognized early on that luxury is not simply about grandeur — it is in the absolute precision of choreography. The exact timing of the sunset vows, the perfect balance of asymmetrical botanical installations, and the smooth flow of guest transport are the details that separate an event from a masterpiece.

Today, our multi-disciplinary team operates from flagship offices in Milan and Santorini, designing, planning, and executing unparalleled celebrations across the Amalfi Coast, southern France, and exclusive island destinations worldwide.

The Atelier Workflow

Our Wedding Planning Process

A deep dive into how we systematically manifest your perfect celebration day.

01 Phase One

Conception & Location

We outline budget matrices, establish destination logistics, and scout private villas, islands, and architectural estates native to your wishes.

02 Phase Two

Custom 3D Rendering

We generate custom digital 3D spatial visualizations of your ceremony site, ensuring perfect height, scale, and shade transitions.

03 Phase Three

Artisan Casting

Our directors book Michelin-rated catering chefs, contract master string quartets, and design customized couture botanical backdrops.

04 Phase Four

The Day-of Direction

Complete production on-site. We manage every supplier, coordinate timelines down to the second, and ensure flawless spatial execution.
